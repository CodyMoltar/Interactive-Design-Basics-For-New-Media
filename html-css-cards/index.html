Theo Jansen

Theo Jansen is a Dutch artist who creates amazing sculptures out of plastic tubes and other materials. He has been making these kinetic sculptures for over 25 years, and they look like living creatures that can move on their own. His work has been featured in museums around the world, and he continues to create new pieces every year. Theo's art is inspiring to people of all ages, especially children who are fascinated by his creations

Rogier van Otterloo

Turks Fruit (1973)

Rogier van Otterloo (1941-1988) was a Dutch composer and conductor who is best known for his film scores. He wrote the music for over 50 films, including The Assault, which won an Academy Award in 1986. He also composed works for orchestra, chamber ensembles, and solo instruments. His style was influenced by jazz and classical music, as well as traditional Dutch folk music. Van Otterloo's work has been praised for its emotional depth and melodic beauty.

Piet Mondrian was a Dutch painter who is best known for his abstract paintings. He began his career as an Impressionist, but later developed a style of painting that featured geometric shapes and primary colors.
